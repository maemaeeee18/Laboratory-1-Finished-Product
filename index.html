Valorant: A tactical Shooter

What is Valorant?

Valorant is a free to play first person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Released in June 2020, it combines elements of tactical shooters and hero-based games.


Key Features

  • Agents: Each character or "Agent", has unique abilities that influence Gameplay.
  • Maps: The game features a variety of maps, each with different strategies and layouts.
  • Weapons: A diverse arsenal of weapon , from rifles to pistols.
